About

Senior Software Engineer with a strong background in distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and platform engineering. Currently at Datadog on the Cloud Cost Management team in Paris, France, working at the intersection of observability and cloud cost optimization.

My experience spans Software Engineering, DevOps, Software Architecture, and Applied Research. I focus on building scalable and reliable systems in complex cloud environments, having worked with event-driven and microservices-based architectures using Python, Golang, and Java across AWS and GCP.

I hold a PhD in Electrical Engineering from UFCG (Brazil) and bring over 15 years of combined industry and academic experience. I am also an Associate Professor (currently on leave) at the Federal Institute of Alagoas.
